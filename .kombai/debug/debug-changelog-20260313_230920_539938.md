## Debug Session: 20260313_230920_539938

## Problem

Tutors and school admins could (via double redirect through `/`) reach each other's dashboards. The middleware redirected unauthorized role to `/` instead of directly to their own dashboard, causing unnecessary round-trips and relying on home-page redirect logic to eventually land them correctly.

## Changes Made

### 1. Improved `/school-dashboard` guard — direct role-based redirect
- **File:** `proxy.ts`
- **Lines:** 132–140 (before)
- **Change:** When a non-SCHOOL_ADMIN hits `/school-dashboard`, redirect them directly to their own dashboard instead of `/`:
  - `TUTOR` → `/tutor`
  - `LEARNER` → `/learner`
  - `ORG_ADMIN` → `/org`
  - others → `/`
- **Revert:** Replace role-specific redirects with a single `return NextResponse.redirect(new URL("/", req.url));`

### 2. Improved `/tutor` guard — direct role-based redirect
- **File:** `proxy.ts`
- **Lines:** 142–148 (before)
- **Change:** When a non-TUTOR hits `/tutor`, redirect them directly to their own dashboard:
  - `SCHOOL_ADMIN` (PENDING) → `/pending-approval`
  - `SCHOOL_ADMIN` (ACTIVE) → `/school-dashboard`
  - `LEARNER` → `/learner`
  - `ORG_ADMIN` → `/org`
  - others → `/`
- **Revert:** Replace role-specific redirects with a single `return NextResponse.redirect(new URL("/", req.url));`
